Fry eggs

Menu

Fast, delicious and easy. The fry eggs, maybe the most easy food in the world, just need a frying pan to make and oil(optional)

No everyone understands the magnificence of this food due to the value for money turn the eggs in a big deal. Either its high protein content or its delicious flavor makes it a highly desirable food.

  • Egg (clearly)
  • Flying pan
  • Oil (optional)
  • Spices (salt, pepper, cumin, thyme, red pepper)
  1. Heat the pan with oil (if you put)
  2. Add the eggs (mixed with the spices if you put)
  3. Wait and enjoy
