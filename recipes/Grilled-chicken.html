Grilled Chicken marinade

Menu

Fast, delicious and easy. The fry eggs, maybe the most easy food in the world, just need a frying pan to make and oil(optional)

No everyone understands the magnificence of this food due to the value for money turn the eggs in a big deal. Either its high protein content or its delicious flavor makes it a highly desirable food.

  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlick powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 5 skinless, bonelles chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk vinegar, soy sauce, olive oil, parsley, basil, oregano, garlic powder, and black pepper together in a bowl.
  3. Pour into a resealable plastic bag. Add chicken, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator, at least 4 hours.
  4. Preheat grill for medium-low heat and lightly oil the grate. Drain and discard marinade.
  5. Grill chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center, 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
